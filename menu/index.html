Serving Westport, New Canaan, Norwalk, CT & Beyond


At Brass & Bone, our menu brings bold flavors and global influences to every dish, from shareable small plates and hearty handhelds to standout entrees and decadent desserts. Enjoy house favorites like Jambalaya Arancini, Etouffee, and catfish with Mediterranean couscous, perfectly paired with craft cocktails and a curated wine list.

Whether you're grabbing bites before the live music starts or lingering over dinner with friends, each plate is crafted to excite your palate and elevate your evening. Call or visit us today. We welcome the Darien, New Canaan, Norwalk, and Westport, CT areas.
