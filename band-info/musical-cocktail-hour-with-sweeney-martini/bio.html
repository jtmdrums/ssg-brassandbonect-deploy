Sweeney Martini is the music-first project created by Jay Sweeney—a songwriter and recording artist whose work is rooted in real-life experience, recovery, and connection. Blending soul, rock, and storytelling, Sweeney Martini moves seamlessly from intimate acoustic duos to full-band performances. At its core is a simple truth that defines the music: “We struggled. We learned. We kept going.”
