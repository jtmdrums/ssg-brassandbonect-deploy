Calliopi is blessed with the absence of any idea that she shouldn't be playing music in front of strangers.

From guitar lessons with her father to finding her way into the Norwalk music scene through participation in the open mics, to booking full gigs and becoming a teacher herself, Calli's journey has been about finding humanity in every song she sings or writes.

Her soulful interpretations and introspections give you what you didn't know you needed.
