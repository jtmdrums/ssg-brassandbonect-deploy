The East Rock Brass Band brings the energy and music of the New Orleans street band tradition to New England. Their stage shows are high-octane, joyous, loud, non-stop parties guaranteed to keep you dancing all night. Since emerging from New Haven in 2023, they have been performing at venues all across Connecticut and Massachusetts. They are also popular in the parade circuit where they bring the infectious feeling of a second line to the streets of the Northeast. An East Rock Brass Band show features the repertoire of New Orleans brass bands (such as Rebirth, Hot 8, Treme, etc.) as well as originals that fuse hip hop, jazz, and latin feels. When they aren't performing, the members of the East Rock Brass Band can usually be found giving back to the community through music education. Firmly believing that music is an important weapon in the fight against injustice, you can also catch them supplying the soundtracks to local protests.
