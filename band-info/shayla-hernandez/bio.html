Shayla Hernandez is an R&B singer/songwriter from Stamford, CT. Her music is compromised of Soul, Funk, Latin and old school elements. With over 15 years of performing under her belt, she captivates her audience through her soulful vocals and relatable lyric content.

Shayla credits artists such as Alicia Keys, Lauryn Hill, Selena and Stevie Wonder as her inspirations. She has opened for musical acts such as Jadakiss, Flo-Rida, Tito Nieves and Elle Varner.
