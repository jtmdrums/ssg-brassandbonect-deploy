Horizon Wireless has blended psychedelic break-beat music with trip-hop, jam, minimal tech house, and electro/trance to create high energy dance sets for the past 16 years using elements from all across the spectrum of sound and time.

Between winning the Grassroots California Mixtape Competition; directly supporting acts like The Crystal Method, RJD2, Hallucinogen, Big Gigantic, The Disco Biscuits, Parov Stelar, BoomBox, The New Deal; and rabidly building a dedicated following all around the Northeast, Horizon Wireless has become an inseparable and integral part of the jam and electronic music scenes.

2018 saw the release of Reimaginate, a 15 track jamband remix album which was met with feverish reviews,. In 2019 Horizon Wireless released the debut original album Welcome to the Network, in 2020 released the Our Nation is Imagination EP. and 2025... the brand new album Three Way Radio hit the airwaves.

With over a decade spanning career consisting of original music, hundreds of live shows, remixes, livestrams, and more, Horizon Wireless is one act that should not be missed.
