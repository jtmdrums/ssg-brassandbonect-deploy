Formed by Matt James, Chris Wilson, and Matt Christoforo, Phatt James channels the essence of iconic rock bands such as Red Hot Chili Peppers and Incubus, formulating a familiar but original sound that serves as an inviting foundation for thoughtful lyrics. This year, the trio has continued to thrill the New York tri-state area with a number of memorable performances and has begun to establish its presence nationally, garnering praise from a number of blogs and podcasts around the United States and receiving airplay on college radio stations such as WQAQ (Quinnipiac University), WNHU (University of New Haven), and WHIP (Temple University).
