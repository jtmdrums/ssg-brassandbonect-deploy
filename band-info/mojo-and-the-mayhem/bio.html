Mojo and The Mayhem started because we like playing music together; that's really the beginning and the end of it. All we want to do is put on great shows with our friends and put out great music that we (and hopefully you) enjoy listening to!
Everyone in the band has a unique style, story, and sense of humor. Aidan and Jimmy, for example, can spend half of band practice quoting The Office at each other, laughing hysterically the whole time. What Im trying to say is, what makes this band so special (if I do say so myself) is the weirdness, the attitude, and the passion for the groove.
