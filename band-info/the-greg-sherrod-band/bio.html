The Greg Sherrod Band is different from your standard bar band; we are the perfect mixture of Blues, Soul, and Rock n' Roll. Being the focal point and driving force for the band's music, Greg Sherrod hand picks musicians to accommodate a wide range of venues. These performers are experienced professional musicians. Greg Sherrod sings for the audience in an unpredictable and wildly entertaining manner. He not only engages his band mates, but every member of the audience as well. We are based in New Haven, Connecticut.
