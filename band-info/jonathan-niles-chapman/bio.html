Jonathan Niles Chapman is a guitarist, singer, and songwriter based in Danbury, Connecticut — and a familiar name on the local scene for more than a decade.

From packed barrooms and backroom stages across the state to gigs around the country, his guitar has traveled far beyond Connecticut. He's toured and played with acts like Poverty Hash, Saint Bernadette, and Guerillafinga, and led his own projects The Last Minutemen, Zen Dudes & the Universe and Three Legged Dog.

Rooted in blues, jazz, and folk, Jonathan writes and plays with a mix of grit and soul; equal parts groove and storytelling. His north stars run from Sam Cooke and Little Richard to Willie Nelson and Bob Dylan; music that swings, stings, and stays with you.
