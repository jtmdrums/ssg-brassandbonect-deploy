Modern Refuge has carved out a unique space in the vibrant music scene of Connecticut and has quickly become one of the most exciting, must-see live acts around. The four-piece band cleverly combines folk warmth with psych-rock sprawl. They are known for their tightly wound indie grooves that rise into orchestral build-ups, harking back to the high-energy acts of the 70s. They have opened for international touring acts including Sammy Hagar and The Circle, George Thorogood & The Destroyers, Talk, and Rozzi. Modern Refuge are putting the final touches on their debut full-length studio album, set for release in early 2025.
