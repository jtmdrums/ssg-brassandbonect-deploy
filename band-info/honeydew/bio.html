The only fruit left in the fruit salad - after all the strawberries have been picked out — is the pale, flavorless honeydew. Yet when it’s ripe, when the stars align, and the season is right, the humble honeydew explodes with a flavor surprising and pure and complex.

Henry Thomas (drums) Gianni Leito (guitar, vocals), Max Perrotti (bass, vocals) and Kiran Edwards (keys, vocals)

believe that a great melon… makes a great jam.
